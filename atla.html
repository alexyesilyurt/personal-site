Avatar: The Last Airbender

This is a thrilling show about the Avatar, the master of all 4 elements: Air, Fire, Water, and Earth . The Avatar, Aang, disappeared for a hundred years, and the Fire Nation took over the world during this time. Then, a brother and a sister, Sokka and Katara, from the water nation find Aang trapped in an iceberg! They free him, as well as his flying bison, Appa. Then, they go on a long, emotional, and exciting journey to free the world from the fire nation!
